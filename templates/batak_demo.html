About Batak

Batak Grill is a beloved Croatian restaurant chain known for its exceptional grilled meats and traditional recipes. With locations across Croatia, we bring the authentic taste of perfectly grilled specialties to every neighborhood.

Our commitment to quality ingredients, expert preparation, and warm hospitality has made us a favorite dining destination for families and friends alike.

  • Premium quality meats
  • Traditional Croatian recipes
  • Family-friendly atmosphere
  • Online reservations available
